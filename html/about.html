About Zakwantu Projects and Services

ZPS is an Eastern Cape based company, owned by a black woman.The company is well-versed in the socio-political landscape of the Eastern Cape province, particularly in the communities of Somerset East, Cookhouse(Blue Crane Route Municipality), Bedford and Adelaide (Raymond Mhlaba Municipality).

Our Objective

Its main objective is to promote and implement education and awareness programmes through training, information-sharing, and dialogue sessions, based on the guidelines and findings of the “National Development Plan”.

Our Philosophy

The company’s philosophy is rooted in a strong passion for the role of developmental and sustainable communities in addressing historical imbalances and advancing the principles of self-sufficiency and empowered communities.

Our Aim

The aim is to bring about long-lasting change in the lives of poor and marginalised communities. Zakwantu PS takes pride in its team, which is fluent in local languages such as isiXhosa, Afrikaans and English.

Our Team

The company has a multi-disciplinary team with the required skills and experience in community development, financial management, stakeholder engagement and management, research, training, and project management.

Our Experience

The team collectively has over 20 years of experience in community development, with 15 years dedicated to schools and educational programs.
